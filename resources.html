Resources

Discover trusted allies and organizations working to combat wildfires. Explore valuable resources and ways to contribute to the cause!

TREE REFORESTATION

Replanting fallen trees and restoring ecosystems is crucial after wildfires. Help preserve nature's balance and support reforestation efforts.

  • Stand For Trees - Protecting forests and climate
  • Green America - Advocating for forests and a sustainable world
  • Team Trees - Join the movement to plant trees globally

HELPING VICTIMS

Supporting victims of wildfires is vital as we face ongoing challenges due to human activity and climate change. Make a difference in their lives.

  • Fire Victim Trust - Providing assistance to fire victims
  • California Fire Foundation - Aiding communities affected by wildfires

CHARITABLE SUPPORT

When donating to charities, be cautious of fraudulent websites. Ensure your contributions have a genuine impact on wildfire relief efforts.

  • The Red Cross - Disaster relief and recovery services
  • Direct Relief - Providing essential medical resources in emergencies
  • The Salvation Army - Assisting disaster survivors

FIRE VOLUNTEERING

Consider becoming a firefighter and contribute directly to firefighting efforts. Your dedication can help save lives and protect communities.

  • The National Volunteer Fire Council - Serving volunteer fire and emergency service organizations
  • Firefighter Opportunities (NVFC) - Discover opportunities to become a firefighter

Most wildfires are preventable. Learn about practices to minimize the risk of wildfires, ensuring safety for communities and nature.

  • Burning Debris - Safe debris burning practices
  • Campfire Safety - Responsible campfire practices
  • Vehicle Maintenance - Safeguarding your vehicle
  • Gun Safety - Safe firearm use
  • Equipment Safety - Responsible equipment use
